## Grouped by Licensing Strategy

### AGPL-3.0 + `ee/` Enterprise (anti-commercialization + open core)
**Cal.com**, **Dub.co**, **Documenso**, **Formbricks**, **Docmost**, **Twenty** (file-level), **NocoDB** (file-level)

### AGPL-3.0 pure (no ee/)
**Firecrawl**, **Postiz**, **Linkwarden**, **Maxun**

### MIT + proprietary EE
**Strapi**, **Rocket.Chat**, **Infisical**, **Activepieces**

### MIT pure
**Hoppscotch**, **NestJS**, **Payload CMS**, **Umami**, **Refine**, **Medusa**, **CopilotKit**, **Better Auth**, **Botpress**, **Scalar**

### Apache-2.0 + `ee/`
**Novu**, **Mastra**, **Amplication**, **Langfuse** (MIT+EE, functionally equivalent)

### Apache-2.0 pure
**Drizzle ORM**, **Backstage**, **Continue**, **Trigger.dev**, **Unleash**

### Source-Available (not OSI-approved)
**n8n** (SUL / fair-code), **Tldraw** (custom), **Remotion** (custom), **Outline** (BSL), **Directus** (BSL)

### Weak copyleft / other
**AFFiNE** (MIT frontend + proprietary backend), **Huly** (EPL-2.0), **Logto** (MPL-2.0), **Budibase** (GPL-3.0 + SQS)

### GPL + proprietary
**Bitwarden** (GPL-3.0 + Bitwarden License for `bitwarden_license/`)
