## Chroma Cloud Pricing

### Starter Plan ($0/month base)
- $5 in free credits
- 10 databases, 10 team members
- Community Slack access
- Usage rates:
  - Write: $2.50/GiB
  - Storage: $0.33/GiB/month
  - Query: $0.0075/TiB + $0.09/GiB returned
  - Fork: $0.03 per call
  - Sync processing: $0.04/GiB
  - Document extraction: $0.01/page
  - Web scraping: $0.01/webpage

### Team Plan ($250/month base)
- $100 usage credits included (don't rollover)
- 100 databases, 30 team members
- Slack support (same-day SLA)
- SOC II compliance
- Volume-based discounts

### Enterprise Plan (Custom)
- Unlimited databases and team members
- Dedicated support
- Single-tenant clusters
- BYOC (Bring Your Own Cloud)
- Custom SLAs

## Monetization Model
Open Core: core vector database is free and open-source. Premium features and managed cloud services drive commercial revenue. Chroma Cloud's serverless managed hosting represents roughly 65% of projected ARR as of early 2026.
