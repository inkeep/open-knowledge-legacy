## Key Facts

- License: AGPLv3 (core), open-core model for cloud
- Stack: Rust backend, Flutter frontend — cross-platform native
- Features: Rich text, databases, Kanban boards, calendars, Markdown editing
- Data: Local-first storage, optional sync, self-hostable
- AI features: Ask questions, writing improvement, brainstorming — but exclusive to paid signed-in users
- Local LLM support: Mistral 7B, Llama 3 via on-device inference
- AI roadmap: "promising but not production-ready in 2026" per comparisons
- No direct equivalent to Notion AI yet
- Free for individuals and small teams
- No MCP support found
- No agent-native features
