## Key Facts

- License: MIT (fully free and open source)
- Stack: PHP, Laravel framework, MySQL
- Content model: Books > Chapters > Pages hierarchy
- Features: WYSIWYG editor, Markdown support, diagrams.net integration, full-text search
- Auth: OIDC, SAML2, LDAP, social auth
- Multilingual: 30+ languages
- Page revisions, image management, role/permission system
- No AI features whatsoever — traditional wiki
- No MCP, no agent integration, no API designed for AI consumption
- Strong self-hosted community but purely a document management tool
