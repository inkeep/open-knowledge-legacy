### Finding: Complete funding history — Seed + Series A, no Series B through April 2026
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Multiple independent databases (Crunchbase, Tracxn, PitchBook, CBInsights)

| Round | Date | Amount | Lead | Participants | Post-Money |
|-------|------|--------|------|--------------|------------|

| Seed | May 2022 | $2.8M | Bain Capital Ventures | TwentyTwo Ventures, Quinn Slack | Not disclosed |

| **Total** | | **$21M** | | | |

Board addition: Jennifer Li and Yoko Li (a16z partners) joined the board at Series A.

Capital efficiency ratio: 4.07x (valuation / total raised), per CBInsights.

**Implications:** The $88.4M post-money at $18M raise means a16z bought ~20.4% of the company. The relatively modest total raise ($21M) for a company now claiming $10M ARR suggests either capital efficiency or undisclosed revenue self-funding.

### Finding: No Series B announced, explored, or reported through April 2026
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED (negative search)
**Evidence:** Searched Crunchbase, Tracxn, PitchBook, CBInsights, TechCrunch, news aggregators — no Series B filing, announcement, or rumor found.

**Implications:** 19 months post-Series A with no new round. If the $10M ARR figure is accurate and the company has been growing at the reported pace, this suggests either (a) they don't need capital yet (sufficient runway), (b) they're waiting for better terms at higher valuation, or (c) they're using M&A to signal momentum for a future raise. All three are plausible; the data cannot distinguish between them.

This could represent 14-48% of the Series A — a wide range of uncertainty that materially affects runway analysis (see B4).

### Finding: Wing VC ET30 #1 Early Stage (March 31, 2026)
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** Wing VC official announcement, BusinessWire, Newcomer reporting

Mintlify ranked #1 in the Early Stage category (companies raised up to $35M) of Wing VC's 2026 Enterprise Tech 30. The ET30 methodology involved 98 venture capitalists across 85 firms managing $2.6 trillion AUM in a two-phase research + survey process.

Other Early Stage honorees: Dust (#2), LlamaIndex, CrewAI, E2B, Crosby Legal, Arcade AI, Pace.

**Implications:** The #1 ranking among 98 VC voters is a strong third-party validation signal. It indicates broad investor awareness and likely investor interest in a future round. However, the ET30 is an awareness/sentiment survey — it does not verify financial performance, unit economics, or product-market fit depth.
