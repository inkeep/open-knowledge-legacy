## Findings

### Finding: Headcount trajectory — 12 → 40 → ~50-55 employees (2024 → 2025 → April 2026)
**Confidence:** INFERRED (moderate-high confidence)
**Evidence:** Multiple sources with some variance

| Period | Headcount | Source | Confidence |
|--------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Early 2024 | 12 | Mintlify 2025 Year in Review | CONFIRMED |
| End 2024 | 18 | Latka | INFERRED |
| End 2025 | 40 | Mintlify 2025 Year in Review | CONFIRMED |
| Feb 2026 | 48 | LeadIQ ("across 4 continents") | INFERRED |
| April 2026 | 50-55 | PitchBook (50), YC (55) | INFERRED |
| April 2026 | 35 | Mintlify careers page | UNCERTAIN (possibly outdated) |

The YC profile (55 employees) and PitchBook (50 employees) are likely the most current. The Mintlify careers page saying "35" may be an older cached number not updated. Tracxn's figure of "11" is clearly stale (Dec 2024 snapshot that predates the year-in-review's "12 → 40" disclosure).

**Implications:** ~4x headcount growth in 18 months (12 → 50+) tracks with the 10x ARR growth. ARR per employee: ~$10M / 50 = $200K — at the lower end of the $150-250K benchmark range for Series A SaaS, but acceptable for a company in aggressive growth mode.
