## Key findings

### Finding: $21M total raised, no Series B through April 2026
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** [Crunchbase](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/mintlify), [PitchBook](https://pitchbook.com/profiles/company/mintlify), [a16z announcement](https://a16z.com/announcement/investing-in-mintlify/), [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2024/09/05/mintlify-is-building-a-next-gen-platform-for-writing-software-docs/). YC W22 ~$500K + Seed $2.8M (Bain Capital Ventures, May 2022) + Series A $18M (a16z, Sep 2024). Post-money: $88.4M per [PremierAlts](https://www.premieralts.com/companies/mintlify/valuation). Capital efficiency ratio: 4.07× per [CBInsights](https://www.cbinsights.com/company/mintlify/financials).
**Implication:** Well-funded at Series A stage but no new capital in 19 months.

### Finding: ~$10M ARR (Sacra estimate, vendor-sourced data)
**Confidence:** INFERRED
**Evidence:** [Sacra profile](https://sacra.com/c/mintlify/) — $10M ARR at end-2025 (10× from ~$1M in 2024). NRR reportedly 150%. [Mintlify 2025 Year in Review](https://www.mintlify.com/blog/2025-year-in-review) — "over 8-figures in ARR."
**Implication:** Strong growth if accurate, but both figures almost certainly originate from Mintlify's own disclosures to Sacra.

### Finding: Named customers include Anthropic, Microsoft, Coinbase, PayPal, AT&T
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** [mintlify.com customers page](https://www.mintlify.com/customers) + case study pages. Named: Anthropic, Cursor, Perplexity, Coinbase, Microsoft, AT&T, PayPal, Zapier, Vercel, Pinecone, Replit, Laravel, ElevenLabs, HubSpot, Scale AI, X (Twitter).
**Implication:** Credibly impressive for a Series A company. Revenue concentration data NOT FOUND.

### Finding: 12 → 50+ employees in 18 months, enterprise-weighted hiring
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** [Mintlify 2025 Year in Review](https://www.mintlify.com/blog/2025-year-in-review) — 12 (early 2024), 40 (end 2025). [PitchBook](https://pitchbook.com/profiles/company/mintlify): 50. [YC Jobs](https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/mintlify/jobs) — 11 open roles including Enterprise AE at $350–420K OTE.
**Implication:** Enterprise AE comp signals $100K+ ACV deals. No layoffs in tech layoff trackers.

### Finding: Wing VC ET30 #1 Early Stage
**Confidence:** CONFIRMED
**Evidence:** [Wing VC ET30 list](https://www.wing.vc/et30/list), [BusinessWire press release](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260331852760/en/) — 98 VCs, 85 firms, $2.6T AUM.
**Implication:** Strong third-party validation signal for Series B fundraising.

### Finding: Estimated remaining cash $4–10M, runway 5–9 months
**Confidence:** UNCERTAIN (high variance from undisclosed acquisitions)
**Evidence:** Burn model: ~50 employees × $250–350K fully loaded = $12.5–17.5M annual headcount + $1–2M infra = $13.5–19.5M total. Revenue offset ~$8–12M. Net burn ~$5.5–7.5M/year (~$460–625K/month). Against $21M raised minus pre-2025 burn minus 2025 burn less revenue minus two undisclosed acquisitions = $4–10M remaining. Per [runway.com benchmarks](https://runway.com/blog/burn-multiple-benchmarks-for-2026-what-good-looks-like-at-seed-to-scale), burn multiple ~1.8× is at-or-slightly-above Series A median.
**Implication:** Series B in 2026 appears necessary unless revenue growth closes the gap.
