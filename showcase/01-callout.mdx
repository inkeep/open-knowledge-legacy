# Callout

The `Callout` descriptor takes GFM alerts, Obsidian callouts, and MDX JSX form — all three parse to the same descriptor and γ-preserve their authoring form.

## GFM alerts (markdown form)

The five canonical types. Source form: `> [!TYPE]\n> Body text`.

> [!NOTE]
> Highlights information that users should take into account, even when skimming.

> [!TIP]
> Optional information to help a user be more successful.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Crucial information necessary for users to succeed.

> [!WARNING]
> Critical content demanding immediate user attention due to potential risks.

> [!CAUTION]
> Negative potential consequences of an action.

## Obsidian foldable syntax

Adding `+` or `-` after the type token produces a collapsible callout. `+` = default-open, `-` = default-closed.

> [!NOTE]+ Default-open callout
> Body is visible on first render; click the title to collapse.

> [!WARNING]- Default-closed callout
> Body is hidden on first render; click the title to expand.

## Alias folding

Non-GFM tokens fold into the 5-type subset (lossy on edit, preserved on pristine save via `data-authored-as`).

<Callout type="tip" data-authored-as="SUCCESS" color="#ffffff">

`success` → folds to `tip`.

</Callout>

> [!DANGER]
> `danger` → folds to `caution`.

> [!IDEA]
> `idea` → folds to `tip`.

## MDX JSX form

Same descriptor, authored as JSX. `title`, `icon`, `color`, `collapsible`, `defaultOpen` are the widened superset props.

<Callout type="note" title="Custom title + icon" icon="lucide:sparkles">
  This callout has a custom title and icon, overriding the type's defaults.
</Callout>

<Callout type="tip" color="#8b5cf6" title="Custom color override">
  The `color` prop overrides the type-derived accent. Useful when brand color differs from semantic accent.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning" collapsible defaultOpen title="Collapsible + defaultOpen">
  This callout is collapsible and starts open. Click the title to toggle.
</Callout>

<Callout type="important" collapsible title="Collapsible, defaultOpen false">
  Collapsible and starts closed. Default state for foldable MDX.
</Callout>

## Nested content

Callouts have `content: 'block*'` — they can contain other block-level content, including other jsxComponents (precedent #9 widening, D1 LOCKED).

<Callout type="note" title="Nested content">
  Callouts support arbitrary block content:

  - Lists
  - **Bold** and _italic_ and `code` and [links](https://example.com)
  - Other descriptors:

  <Accordion title="Nested accordion" defaultOpen>
    An Accordion inside a Callout. γ walks descendants and preserves their authoring form independently.
  </Accordion>
</Callout>
